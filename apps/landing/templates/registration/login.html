Dozo

Dozo is a data-driven
productivity & goal-targeting tool
created to help students improve their
work efficiency by habituating a
continuous flow of uninterrupted work.

With Dozo, you can:
  • Plan a work session with time estimates for each task
  • Analyze task completion with instantaneous feedback
  • Measure your growth alongside of other platform users via a quantifiable points system
  • Visualize your progress with data-rich dashboards that show your session and task history*

* Still a work in progress
